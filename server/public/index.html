Our Story



Founded in celebration of all that a great cup of coffee offers the senses and ignites joy, in 2023 we opened the doors to our first cafe in Charlotte. From day one we have approached every decision with intention, purpose and whimsy - from the recipes we create, to the spaces we curate. We want ours to be a place of community, comfort and nourishment.
